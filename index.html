About

The bridge between football and data

I have worked across the pathway – with academy players of all ages to the senior team – as a coach, head coach and analyst. That on‑pitch experience is combined with an academic background: a Master's in Computer Science focused on data science, AI and model interpretability.

In many clubs, data exists but the real challenge is making sense of it. My strength lies in storytelling with data: connecting what we see in the numbers and on the video with what players and staff feel on the grass. I prioritise clarity, context and communication so that insights are actually used.

Soft skills are central to my work: clear communication, leadership and man‑management. I am equally comfortable in the analyst room, on the training pitch or presenting to technical staff and boards.

Approach

How we work together

My goal is always the same: connect your reality on the pitch with the right use of data, in a language your staff and players trust.

01

Understand your context

We start with your environment: level, resources, game model, staff, current data and workflows. This ensures that any ideas fit your reality, not an abstract ideal.

02

Analyse & interpret

I combine data, video and qualitative information to identify key questions and patterns. The focus is on interpretability and relevance, not just complexity.

03

Translate into football decisions

Insights are turned into clear actions: changes in training, tactical adjustments, recruitment decisions, or individual development plans – all explained in football language.

04

Support implementation

I stay involved to help you apply and refine the work, whether that means being on the pitch, in the analysis room or in online meetings with staff.
